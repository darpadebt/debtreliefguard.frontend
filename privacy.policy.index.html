Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 28, 2025

1. Who We Are

DebtReliefGuard.com is an independent educational website that helps consumers compare debt relief programs. We are not a lender or settlement provider. We partner with verified, FTC-compliant services and use secure form technology provided by GoHighLevel.

2. What Information We Collect

  • Voluntarily submitted form data (name, phone, email, debt amount, etc.)
  • IP address, location (city/state), device/browser info (for compliance)
  • Scroll behavior, clicks, heatmaps (via AI optimizer & analytics)

3. How We Use It

  • To provide you with a free, secure debt relief estimate
  • To route submissions to a single verified partner (no mass selling)
  • To audit and improve site content and performance via ChatGPT-powered AI
  • To ensure TCPA and federal compliance by logging timestamp, IP, and consent

4. Your Rights

You can request to view, delete, or restrict your data at any time. We do not sell, lease, or rent your personal information. All requests can be sent to info@gatewaydebthelp.com.

5. Cookie & Tracking Policy

We use cookies and session storage for analytics, compliance logging, A/B testing, and AI loopback optimization. No third-party advertising cookies are used. You may disable cookies in your browser at any time.

6. Form Technology & Encryption

Forms are securely embedded via GoHighLevel, encrypted in transit, and hosted through verified sources. Submission data is only shared with the selected provider for processing and will not be sold or transferred.

7. California, Oregon, and Restricted States

Users from CA, WI, WV, RI, and OR may browse the site but cannot submit lead forms. If submitted, those entries will be logged and respectfully declined in accordance with state laws.

8. Updates

This policy may be updated automatically through AI compliance auditing. Last revision is always noted above. Continued use of this site implies acceptance of the current version.
