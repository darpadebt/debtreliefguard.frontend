Debt Relief Fraud Prevention: Common Red Flags in Debt Relief Scams

Recognizing scam tactics is vital for debt relief fraud prevention. Here are some common red flags to watch out for:

  • Unsolicited Offers: If you're contacted out of the blue by a company offering debt relief services, be wary. Legitimate companies typically do not cold-call customers.
  • Pressure to Act Quickly: Scammers often create a false sense of urgency to push you into making hasty decisions. Legitimate providers will give you time to consider your options.
  • Requests for Upfront Fees: Most legitimate debt relief services will not ask for payment until they have performed a service. Be cautious of any company that demands payment before any work is done.
