Secure Data Handling Practices

When selecting a debt relief provider, be wary of red flags that indicate poor data protection practices. This means they will obtain your explicit consent before accessing or sharing your information, ensuring you remain in control of your data. It also means they will be transparent about how your data is collected, used, and shared, allowing you to make informed decisions about your participation in their programs.
