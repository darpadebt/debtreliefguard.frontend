Watch Out for Debt Relief Scams

If it sounds too good to be true, it probably is. Below are real red flags based on Federal Trade Commission and CFPB warnings.

See official CFPB warning

🚫 Upfront Fees

Legit programs never charge upfront. It’s illegal under the FTC Telemarketing Sales Rule.

📵 No Contact Info

If you can't find a real address or phone number, walk away. Legitimate companies don’t hide.

🧪 “Guaranteed” Results

No debt relief can guarantee savings. If they promise to remove all debt without review — it’s a scam.
