Your Primary Options

Review the pros/cons at a glance. If you’d like, we’ll map them to your situation in about a minute.

💸 Debt Settlement

Negotiate to resolve accounts for less than you owe. Goal: reduce balances and finish faster than minimums.

  • Best for: $10k+ unsecured debt; hardship or high minimums
  • Consider: Temporary credit impact; funds set aside for settlements
  • FTC rule: No fees until a debt is settled

💳 Debt Consolidation

Replace multiple debts with one lower-rate loan or balance transfer to simplify payments.

  • Best for: Good credit + stable income
  • Pros: Potentially lower monthly payment and interest
  • Cons: Requires approval; risk of re-borrowing if habits don’t change

⚖️ Bankruptcy

Legal reset for overwhelming debt; serious but sometimes necessary.

  • Chapter 7: Discharge if you qualify
  • Chapter 13: Court-approved repayment plan
  • Impact: Public record; credit effects 7–10 years

📊 Debt Management Plan (DMP)

Organized repayment via an agency; interest concessions and budgeting support.

  • Best for: Moderate credit card debt; steady income
  • Notes: One monthly payment; fees vary by provider
