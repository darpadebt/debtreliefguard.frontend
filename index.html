Frequently asked

Will this hurt my credit?
Requesting an estimate here does not impact your credit. We don’t run a hard inquiry.

How is this different from bankruptcy?
We focus on non-bankruptcy options (settlement, consolidation, counseling) to reduce payments and resolve unsecured debt without a court filing.

Do I still pay my creditors?
It depends on the option. We’ll explain how payments flow (to a program account vs. creditors) so you understand timing and tradeoffs.

What is your fee structure?
FTC-aligned: no fees until your first debt is successfully settled. Fees and terms are disclosed in writing before you enroll.
